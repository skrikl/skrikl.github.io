About

Hello, I'm Sergio K, an IT Project Manager and Frontend Developer with over a decade of experience. My passion lies in making the internet accessible to everyone. I believe that a well-designed user interface can and should be inclusive, catering to the needs of all users, regardless of their abilities or circumstances.

In the digital world, we can never fully anticipate the unique needs of every user. This is why I strive to follow best practices in accessibility, ensuring that the projects I manage and develop are usable by as many people as possible. I am committed to understanding and addressing the diverse needs of users, and to continuously learning and adapting as those needs evolve.

My technical skills include frontend development with modern frameworks like Angular, always with a focus on accessibility. I believe that accessibility is not just about catering to specific groups of users with disabilities. Instead, it's about improving the user interface for everyone. A more accessible interface is a more usable interface, leading to a better user experience for all. I also bring this commitment to accessibility to my project management methodologies, including Scrum and Kanban, ensuring that accessibility is a priority from the start to the finish of every project.
